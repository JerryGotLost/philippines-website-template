Overview

  • Terrain Description: Mostly mountains with narrow to extensive coastal lowlands.
  • Population: 112,508,994
  • Typical climate: “The Climate of the Philippines is tropical and maritime. It is characterized by relatively high temperature, high humidity and abundant rainfall. It is similar in many respects to the climate of the countries of Central America.” - https://www.worldometers.info/world-population/philippines-population/
  • Natural hazards (Problem): The Philippines is the most hazardous countries in the world. The biggest water related hazard is flooding. Learn more about water flooding in the Philippines at Floodlist.com
  • Social issues (How it effects people): Floods villages causing shelters to collapse, farms overfill with water causing crops dying, etc.

As you can see, the Philippines is already showing signs of being affected by climate change (The uh flooding).
